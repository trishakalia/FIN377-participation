</section><h2 class="section-heading" onclick="javascript:mfTempOpenSection(1)"><div class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element indicator mw-ui-icon-small mw-ui-icon-flush-left"></div><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mid-America_Apartment_Communities&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History" data-section="1" class="mw-ui-icon mw-ui-icon-element mw-ui-icon-minerva-edit-enabled edit-page mw-ui-icon-flush-right">Edit</a></span></h2><section class="mf-section-1 collapsible-block" id="mf-section-1">
<p>The company was founded in 1977 by George E. Cates.<sup id="cite_ref-cates_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cates-3">[3]</a></sup></p><p>In 1993, the company was organized as a real estate investment trust.<sup id="cite_ref-10K_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10K-1">[1]</a></sup></p><p>In February 1994, the company acquired The Cates Company and became a <a href="/wiki/Public_company" title="Public company">public company</a> via an <a href="/wiki/Initial_public_offering" title="Initial public offering">initial public offering</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-cates_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-cates-3">[3]</a></sup></p><p>In January 2004, the company acquired properties in <a href="/wiki/Dallas,_Texas" class="mw-redirect" title="Dallas, Texas">Dallas, Texas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nashville,_Tennessee" title="Nashville, Tennessee">Nashville, Tennessee</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup></p><p>In 2011, the company acquired 256-unit apartment community in <a href="/wiki/Savannah,_Georgia" title="Savannah, Georgia">Savannah, Georgia</a><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> and a 300-unit apartment community in <a href="/wiki/Richmond,_Virginia" title="Richmond, Virginia">Richmond, Virginia</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup></p><p>In October 2013, the company acquired <a href="/wiki/Colonial_Properties" title="Colonial Properties">Colonial Properties</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p><p>In 2016, the company was added to the <a href="/wiki/S%26P_500_Index" title="S&amp;P 500 Index">S&amp;P 500 Index</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup></p><p>In 2016, the company acquired Alexan Heights, a 352-unit apartment complex in <a href="/wiki/Houston_Heights" title="Houston Heights">Houston Heights</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-alexan_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-alexan-2">[2]</a></sup></p><p>In December 2016, the company acquired <a href="/wiki/Post_Properties" title="Post Properties">Post Properties</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup></p>
