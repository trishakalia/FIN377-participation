As of 2018, the company owns over $20 billion in assets.

</p><p>In 1985, starting as Healthcare Property Investors, the company became publicly listed through an <a href="/wiki/Initial_public_offering" title="Initial public offering">Independent Public Offering (IPO)</a>. The company has grown into a leading healthcare REIT and earned its way into the <a href="/wiki/S%26P_500_Index" title="S&amp;P 500 Index">S&amp;P 500 index</a> in 2008. Healthpeak is a Maryland corporation and is headquartered in <a href="/wiki/Irvine,_California" title="Irvine, California">Irvine, California</a>, with offices in <a href="/wiki/Nashville,_Tennessee" title="Nashville, Tennessee">Nashville</a> and <a href="/wiki/San_Francisco" title="San Francisco">San Francisco</a>.
</p><p>Thomas M. Herzog currently serves as the company's <a href="/wiki/Chief_executive_officer" title="Chief executive officer">chief executive officer</a> and is a member of the <a href="/wiki/Board_of_directors" title="Board of directors">board of directors</a>. He served as HCP's <a href="/wiki/Chief_financial_officer" title="Chief financial officer">chief financial officer</a> after rejoining the company in 2016. Scott M. Brinker was named the company's <a href="/wiki/President_(corporate_title)" title="President (corporate title)">president</a> and <a href="/wiki/Chief_investment_officer" title="Chief investment officer">chief investment officer</a> (CIO) in October 2019; he had previously served as executive vice president and CIO since March 2018.
</p>
